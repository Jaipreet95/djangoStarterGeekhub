Create a virtualenv
    1) Using virtualenv 
        Go to the folder where your environment resides
        ```virtualenv <name of env>```
        ```cd <name of env>/bin```
        ```source activate```

    2) Using virtualenvwrapper
          mkvirtualenv -> creates a virtual env  
          ```mkvirtualenv <name of env> ```  
          rmvirtualenv
          setvirtualenvproject
